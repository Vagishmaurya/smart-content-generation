DNA is the blueprint of life - a double helix structure containing all the genetic information needed to build and maintain every living organism. This remarkable molecule is at the heart of sexual reproduction, ensuring that genetic information is passed from parents to offspring with beautiful variations that drive evolution.
üî¨ Key Concepts
  • Double helix structure with complementary base pairs
  • Contains genetic instructions for all traits
  • Undergoes replication before cell division
  • Mutations create genetic variation
Amazing Fact!
If you unraveled all the DNA in your body and laid it end to end, it would stretch about 10 billion miles - enough to reach from Earth to Pluto!
